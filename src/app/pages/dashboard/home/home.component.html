About the program

Gujarat Technological University and L&T EduTech signed an MoU to offer niche industry-abridged certification courses through the L&T EduTech platform to the students of . Under this partnership, L&T EduTech will offer application-oriented skill development courses in cohorts with GTU for the students of GTU and its affiliated colleges.

With both partners on a mission to bridge the skill gap faced by industry and academia through industry readiness programs for students, this joint initiative is aimed at offering programs built at all levels - from Foundation to Practitioner. Powered by L&T’s decades-long experience in Engineering and Technology, these courses are focused on empowering graduating students and improving their employability quotient through a strong industry-academia interface that connects theory and practice.

About GTU

Gujarat Technological University (GTU), a premier academic and research institution established by the government of Gujarat.A state university with about 400,000 students and 486 affiliated colleges across the state of Gujarat, GTU offers courses in engineering, architecture, management, pharmacy and computer science.

About L&T EduTech

L&T EduTech is an initiative under Larsen & Toubro to bridge the gap between Industry and Academia. The core objective is to enhance the employability of students and career progression for professionals across Engineering & Technology sectors. L&T offers a series of industry-led, application-oriented programmes under its portfolio – L&T CollegeConnect, Vocational Skilling, Professional Certification, Assessments & Certifications and Skills Exchange.
